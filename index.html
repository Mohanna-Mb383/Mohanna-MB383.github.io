Research Topic

Graph Neural Networks and Topological Data Analysis for phylogenetic inference, with a focus on bacterial datasets and scalable methods under missing data and evolutionary complexity.

Short Description

I work on GNN- and transformer-based models for bacterial phylogenetics, focusing on sequence encoding (cgMLST/SNP, gap-aware), graph and axial-attention architectures, and robustness to missing data. I am also exploring generative ideas inspired by methods such as PHYLOVAE.
